Ahmad Al-Halabi is a Palestinian painter who believes that the dreams destroyed in war can be replaced with other dreams, including selling his paintings and sharing them with the world. His paintings are not just ink on paper, but every feeling that he is unable to express. If you want to understand the painter Ahmad Al-Halabi, you must see his paintings. You will understand every dream he aspired to, every pain, and every hope that still clings to him. Therefore, you must remember that every painting you buy has a story and feelings...
